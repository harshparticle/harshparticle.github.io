Harsh Aryan Jalan
PhD Student, Theoretical & Computational Chemistry
University of Southern California

I am a second-year PhD student in theoretical and computational chemistry at the University of Southern California. My research focuses on non-radiative decay processes in organic chromophores, including internal conversion and intersystem crossing, with an emphasis on vibronic coupling and rate theories.

Before joining USC, I completed my MS at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay and my BS at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra.
